Doctors

Michael S. Mall, MD

Name: Michael S. Mall, MD
Statement: Dr. Michael Mall is the cofounder and medical director of New Image Advanced Laser Skin Center in Las Vegas, with well over three decades in practice.

Dr. Mall’s full service laser skin care center provides patients with a wide range of nonsurgical laser and aesthetic treatment options for the face and body. These include dermal fillers and injectables like Juvéderm and Botox to smooth skin and restore lost volume, as well as skin tightening, IPL Photofacials, body contouring and fat reduction using the truSculpt laser, micro peels, skin resurfacing, and laser hair removal.

Dr. Mall earned his medical degree from the University of Arizona College of Medicine, after which he completed a residency at Phoenix Baptist Hospital.

Today, he is passionate about offering the most advanced technology and techniques, such as radiofrequency and infrared skin tightening, the PicoWay laser for tattoo removal, and the O-shot, P-shot, and FemTouch laser for sexual health and rejuvenation.

“There isn’t a part of the body we can’t treat,” says Dr. Mall. He and his highly trained staff offer customized consultations and treatment plans to help patients achieve their goals.

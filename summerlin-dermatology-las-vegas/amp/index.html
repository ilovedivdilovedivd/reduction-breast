Doctors

Reuel Aspacio, MD, FAAD

Name: Reuel Aspacio, MD, FAAD
Statement: Dr. Reuel Aspacio is a board-certified dermatologist and the founder and medical director of Summerlin Dermatology in Las Vegas, Nevada.

“You deserve the finest quality and highest value of care available,” says Dr. Aspacio. “I strive to provide service that is attentive, friendly, and complete. Our office setting is professional yet informal, and lends itself to a comfortable and warm doctor-patient relationship.”

His diverse background and multilingual staff ensures that Dr. Aspacio and his team can provide the best medical care to an array of patients from all ethnicities and ages.

Dr. Aspacio’s specialities include surgical and nonsurgical cosmetic and medical dermatological treatments, including laser rejuvenation, cosmetic injectables, skin tightening, fat reduction, sclerotherapy, skin cancer screenings, platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy for hair loss, and managing chronic conditions like melasma, psoriasis, eczema, and acne.

Dr. Aspacio earned his medical degree from University of California Davis School of Medicine in Sacramento. After graduation, he completed an internship in Internal Medicine at the University of California in San Francisco, followed by a residency in Dermatology at Martin Luther King, Jr./Charles Drew Medical Center in Los Angeles.

Board certified by the American Board of Dermatology, Dr. Aspacio is a member of the American Society of Cosmetic Dermatology and Aesthetic Surgery, the National Psoriasis Foundation, and the Las Vegas Dermatological Society. He is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.

Dr. Aspacio is also an associate professor at the University of Nevada, School of Medicine University Medical Center. He has presented and lectured at many national conferences and conventions on the topic of cosmetic and medical dermatology, including well-regarded research around the topic of acne and the use of laser technology in the treatment of acne scarring.

He has been the recipient of numerous awards and professional accolades over his more than 25 years in practice. He has been named the Top Doctor in the city by Las Vegas Magazine, as well as one of their “Top 10 Doctors in Nevada,” “Top 10 Doctors in Las Vegas,” and “Top 10 Doctors in the Metro Area, Greater Las Vegas” for dermatology.

He is also an American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery Expert Injector, a distinction reserved only for the 3% of doctors in the U.S. who display the most skilled application, reliable results, and thorough patient education.

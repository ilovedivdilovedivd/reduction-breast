Does a person with I cup have the chance to be a C cup?
Breast reduction
Mar 28, 2019
It is difficult if not impossible to predict a specific bra cup size when performing a breast reduction.  Bra cups are not standardized, so one manufacturers C may be another's D or DD.  In general, I tell patients that they will be smaller, and more proportionate to the rest of their body.  You should try not to focus on a specific bra size.  If you are happy with the size of your breasts and your bra says D, does it matter?

Is it normal to have rippling 4 days after revision surgery? Sientra smooth round moderate plus 575c
Rippling after breast augmentation
Feb 19, 2019
Rippling can be visible when there is insufficient soft tissue covering the implants, and/or there is a discrepancy between the pocket size and the implant size.  In your case, it looks like you probably have insufficient soft tissue covering the implant.  Fat grafting would be an option.  Off the shelf fillers can also help, but may be considerably more expensive if a large volume is needed.

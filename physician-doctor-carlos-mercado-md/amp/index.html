Is it normal to have fluid build up in my abs after Vaser Liposcution?
Vaser Post Treatment Massages
Jun 15, 2018
Good Morning,
At our office we recommend patients to go for lymphatic draining massages at around twelve weeks post op. The patient generally does around ten sessions (one per week).
It is still possible to have fluid retention at five months due to the fact that a large volume of fluid is used during the procedure in order to numb the area, and use the ultrasound component.

Should I consider more fillers to fix my uneven eyelids?
Check with an oculoplastic surgeon or facial plastic surgeon
Aug 2, 2020
Hi beauty, Injecting fillers in your area of concern is very high risk and can lead to some pretty severe consequences. I would recommend that you make an appointment with an oculoplastic surgeon or facial plastic surgeon prior to getting any more fillers to your eyelids. You may be surprised with the surgical options they have, that can often times be done with local anesthesia and little downtime. Best of luck, beauty!

How long after or before lasers to get tear troughs filler and glabella Botox?
A small break is needed
Aug 2, 2020
Hi, love. In general, I typically recommend waiting two weeks between procedures (injectables and lasers). This will help your body recover from the laser and/or allow the botox to kick in prior to your next treatment. Of course, I always consider things on a case by case basis, so check with you injector or esthetician before scheduling your appointments! Best of luck!

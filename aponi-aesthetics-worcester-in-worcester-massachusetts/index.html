Bellafill rarely causes lumps
Sep 20, 2019
Bellafill is an excellent product for chin and smile lines! We use a lot of Bellafill in our practice,  and I very rarely see lumps. Studies actually show reduced incidence of complications like lumps with Bellafill compared to other fillers. The advantage of Bellafill is the 5 year duration. How long does Restalyne Defyne last in the lips?
Does Defyne last longer in the lips
Jun 18, 2019
In my experience, Defyne does tend to last longer in the lips. I attribute this to the superior elasticity of the product, which allows it to move with your mouth muscles, so it does not get broken down as fast. I find (in general) lip fillers last 6-12 months, and Defyne lasts 8-15 months. I have a subset of patients for whom all of the fillers last for 6 months, and I believe - as Dr. Emers notes in his reply - that youth and activity level can affect duration of filler.

Swollen area after filler injection

Your question does not state how long ago you had the filler injected, but if it has been at least 2 weeks and it looks asymmetric or fuller on one side then you should go back to your injector, who can treat it. Defyne works very well in the chin area, and offers good support, but if more was put on one side it might appear fuller. Does excessive intake of alcohol cause faster breakdown of dermal fillers?
Does alcohol use change dermal filler duration?
Jun 18, 2019
I have never found any evidence that alcohol consumption has an effect on filler duration. I have found some people in whom filler does not seem to last as long as others, but  I think it is multifactorial and related to one's genetic make-up.

How many ml for my nasolabial folds?
Filler recommendations
Dec 6, 2020
I would recommend 0.5 ml of hyaluronic acid filler for each fold for a total of 1.0ml. This will help soften these areas without overfilling them.
Where is the most effective place for filler injections?
Filler recommendations
Dec 6, 2020
I would recommend hyaluronic acid filler in the nasolabial folds, marionette lines and cheekbones. Would recommend 1-2 syringes for each area.
Do you recommend treatment straight into the folds and or into the cheeks?
Hyaluronic Filler in Nasolabial folds
Aug 24, 2020
I would recommend hyaluronic filler injected subdermally into the nasolabial folds. I think your cheeks have adequate volume and projection and would not recommend filler into the cheek area.
How can I smooth my forehead area/fill in the tear trough area?
Forehead and Tear Troughs
Aug 17, 2020
I would recommend either a firm hyaluronic acid filler(Volluma/Restylane Lyft) or Radiesse for the forehead and temple areas. For the tear troughs I prefer to use Restylane L.
What procedure can you suggest for my aging skin? I'm 51 years old.
CO2 laser resurfacing
Aug 5, 2020
Generally wrinkles that occur with expression are best treated with neuromodulator such as Botox and Dysport. Wrinkles that are present at rest are best treated with a resurfacing procedure such as laser resurfacing, chemical peeling or dermabrasion. In your case I would recommend CO2 laser resurfacing. Chemical peeling would also work well, particularly around the eyes and mouth.
Very obvious nasolabial folds and marionette lines at 29. Options?
hyaluronic acid filler vs SMAS grafting
Jul 29, 2020
Everyone tends to have at least some mild degree of asymmetry to the face thus the reason one fold may appear deeper than another. The first and easiest option would be subdermalinjection of hyaluronic acid filler to these areas. Another more long lasting option would be to graft some of your own collagen to these areas to act as a natural filler. This is usually harvested from a small incision behind the ear.
Will natural lip filler suit my face?
yes to lip filler
Jul 29, 2020
I think that a conservative amount of hyaluronic acid filler injected precisely and appropriately to uour upper and lower lips would look great. If done appropriately it would not produce an “off balance”look and merely enhance your natural appearance.

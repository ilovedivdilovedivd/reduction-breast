What’s the good time for second surgery? Having rhinoplasty in Feb. want to book a BBL soon after
Do both
Jan 3, 2018
You can easily undergo both surgeries at the same time with an experienced plastic surgeon. Make sure you see pictures of both procedures and don’t be fooled by only a profile photo of the nose. That’s the easiest one to look good. With your breast lift make sure your surgeon does the lollipop technique. You don’t need that bottom scar and it will restrict what type of clothing you can wear. Good luck.
Was my rhino septoplasty a mistake? 5 years post op.
Secondary rhino
Sep 21, 2017
You need grafts placed along the lower part of your nose called lateral crural strut grafts. It will improve your breathing and it will look better. I wouldn’t return to your original surgeon. Seek someone out with experience in secondary rhinoplasty.
Do I need a breast lift for borderline ptosis?
Breast lift
Sep 18, 2017
You are not borderline only with your hands behind your back does it look that way. You need a vertical lift. Lollipop with a round implant don’t let someone talk you into the periareolar lift it won’t work for you.
What sort of Abdominoplasty procedure would be best?
Abdominal wall shaping
Mar 12, 2014
You are not a candidate for a mini abdomenoplasty . You need to reshape your torso to give a more feminine appearance . The best type of tummy tuck for this is the high lateral tension abdomenoplasty . You can get rid of bra rolls and tighten inner and outer thighs all with the abdomenoplasty incision. You can also have lipo of any of these areas for shaping if you need it. If you are shopping make sure you see a lot of photos of their work including all views even from behind ….

Will Muscle Tightening During TT Help with Hour Glass Figure?
Muscle tightening during abdominoplasty
Mar 5, 2012
Results for a tummy tuck can vary widely depending on who does your surgery and what technique . I suggest for you to have an extended abdominalplasty using a high lateral tension technique .

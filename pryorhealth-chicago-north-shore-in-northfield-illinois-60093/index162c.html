Mohammed Asif, MD

Name: Mohammed Asif, MD
Statement: Dr. Mohammed Asif received his Doctor of Medicine degree from the American University of Integrated Sciences and his bachelor’s degree in Biological Sciences with honors from Bowie State University. After completing his General Surgery residency at Mercy Catholic Medical Center, Drexel University School of Medicine, he pursued a Burn Surgery Fellowship at the John Hopkins Burn Center. There, he participated in several active clinical research trials and published more than 30 articles as an Instructor and later as a Clinical Assistant Professor in the Department of Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery.

Dr. Asif is a member of numerous prestigious medical organizations, including the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, The Illinois Society of Plastic Surgeons, The American College of Surgeons, and The Association for Academic Surgery. He has been featured in the John Hopkins Magazine and on both CBS and Fox, discussing his surgical techniques and views on a wide range of patient care topics.

Dr. (Mo) Asif has years of meticulous reconstructive surgical experience that has served as a basis for his unparalleled aesthetic surgical skills. His ability to reconstruct even the most compromised tissue enables him to deliver outstanding results to those in need of a wide array of aesthetic care, including Mommy Makeovers, Breast Augmentation, Abdominoplasty, Liposuction, and Body Contouring.

Dr. Asif provides the same level of care in an aesthetic practice as he does in an acute setting. He is proud to deliver life-changing results that boost self-esteem and help his patients feel comfortable and confident about themselves.

When not practicing, Dr. Asif enjoys family time and the outdoors with his wife and three young children.

I am very self conscious about my butt. What can I do?
Non-Surgical Buttock Augmentation
Sep 4, 2020
There are many non-surgical procedures for the buttock augmentation.Depending on what you are looking to improve, different types of procedures and devices might be used, including: semi permanent biostimulators, such as Radiesse and Sculptra to improve the skin elasticity and add volume, PDO or PLLA threads to lift the tissue, smooth PDO threads to fill in the dimples and improve cellulite, microneedling devices to promote the collagen stimulation and improve the skin elasticity.
What are non surgical procedures for bigger booty?
Sculptra - Non Surgical BBL
Sep 4, 2020
Sculptra is the most popular procedure for non-surgical buttock augmentation to enhance,contour and shape the buttocks. It stimulates your skin’s own natural collagen production, working over time to enhance the shape of the buttocks. The results are gradual and last up to 2-3 years. Typically 3 treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart are needed.
How to fix my banana roll/double crease/unpronounced butt area?
Kybella for dissolving fat
Sep 4, 2020
Kybella injections are FDA approved and safe non-surgical treatments that dissolve the fat permanently. You may need 2-6 treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart to see a significant reduction of “banana rolls”.
Best options to lift and tighten? Add fat, Sculptra, Renuva, or PRP?
Sculptra
Sep 4, 2020
Sculptra, Hyperdiluted Radiesse, and PDO threads are the best non-surgical options to improve texture and skin elasticity to tighten and lift the sagging tissue.
How can I remove the scarring, dimples and shape loss on my buttocks?
Improving skin texture, cellulite and dimpled skin
Sep 4, 2020
Biostimulators, such as Hyperdiluted Radiesse and Sculptra can significantly improve skin texture, cellulite and dimpled skin. Smooth PDO threads is the other option to address individual dimples. Microneedling is a safe procedure to improve the scars and promote your own collagen.

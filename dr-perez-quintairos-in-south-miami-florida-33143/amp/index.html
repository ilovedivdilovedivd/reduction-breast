Can I get lip injections (Belotero) whilst breastfeeding my 8-month-old baby?
No
Jun 15, 2021
Hello, we recommend not to get any injectables while you are pregnant or nursing because there has not been sufficient studies to know whether its safe. Also, fillers have not been approved for use during that time.
Dysport or lactic acid peel first?
Yes, you can do both procedures on the same day
Jun 15, 2021
You can get a chemical peel the same day as Dysport. We recommend the peel first because you cannot lie down after Dysport for 4 hours. Another reason for the peel to go first is that the needle entry points will allow more of the peel acid to penetrate more than what is intended which would not be recommended.

Salt Lake City Reduction Mammoplasty

Reduction mammoplasty (breast reduction) in Salt Lake City is an operation aimed at reducing the volume of the mammary glands with the possible correction of breast ptosis or asymmetry.

The operation for breast reduction is under general anesthesia, its duration is 2.5-4 hours.

During surgery, the surgeon removes excess skin, resets the planned volume of glandular and adipose tissue, tightens the breast, giving it the desired shape and symmetry, taking into account all the wishes of the patient.

Standard reduction mammoplasty in Salt Lake City, UT is performed with the movement of the nipple-areola complex followed by the application of cosmetic sutures and special strips (such as Steri-strip and Micropore).

The patient stays in the hospital for 2-3 days after the operation. Sutures are removed on the 10-12th day of the postoperative period.

Reasons for breast reduction

Breast reduction is associated with the medical concept of macromastia, which describes an excessively large breasts. Macromastia is one of the abnormalities of the development of the mammary glands.

Many women suffer from excessive breast size. They often feel, that because of the large size of their breasts they are constantly being considered by other people. Often this leads to an involuntary curvature of the thoracic spine in order to hide the breast.

With a large breast size, conservative therapy does not make sense because physical exercises and psychotherapy do not eliminate the real problem – a significant weight of the breast.

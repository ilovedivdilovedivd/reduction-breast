Dr. Rivera got her undergraduate degree from the University of Puerto Rico, then went on to pursue a degree in medicine at UCC School of Medicine, also in Puerto Rico. She did her residency in Internal Medicine and her fellowship training in Endocrinology at the University of South Florida in Tampa. In addition to her medical expertise, Dr. Rivera has extensive training in alternative medicine, allowing her to design treatment plans that combine the best of both fields.

She believes each patient is unique and deserves a comprehensive, customized treatment plan that suits their needs and concerns. She also speaks for multiple renowned aesthetic organizations like the American Med Spa Association (AMSPA) and is the Founder of the GLANZ Aesthetics Academy which trains other providers about aesthetic treatments and Laser Safety.

Being Hispanic, she has passionately learned about the different ethnic skin tones and how to treat them safely and effectively with different laser modalities. We are not all created equal therefore she designs treatments that are unique for each patient. Her goal is to provide an ambiance of healing and wellness, while bringing out the best of YOU!

360 lipo + BBL. Can I get breast augmentation 2 weeks later?
BBL and Breast
Mar 13, 2023
Thank you for your question. Generally we do not recommend the two procedures so close to together. If you have your BBL first- most recommendations include sleeping on your stomach for the first 4-6 weeks. This can cause additional pressure on a new breast augmentation causing displacement of the implants, wound healing issues and poor cosmetic results. It is best to stage the procedures 8-12 weeks apart.

Jan 15, 2019
It's a little hard to see the moles in the photo, but in general, careful surgical removal will permanently get rid of these lesions and leave a very acceptable scar. Plastic surgeons are experts at removing things of this nature and orienting the scar to follow the natural skin creases we all have. Another option, but one that requires two separate surgeries is to have each mole removed by cutting just beyond the border of each then allowing the circular hole to heal by itself....
Do I qualify for the mini tummy tuck?
It depends...
Jan 15, 2019
It depends on what your goals are. If you go ahead and have a mini tummy tuck (and maybe some upper abdominal liposuction) you will see significant improvement. However, if you want maximum improvement, you would need a traditional tummy tuck (abdominoplasty). This would allow the maximum amount of redundant skin to be removed and the abdominal wall can be tightened as well, which cannot really be done in a mini tummy tuck.

If a nose has been broken is there always visible damage or scarring?
not necessarily...
Oct 21, 2013
It depends of how badly the nose was broken. If the bones were broken, but not displaced, usually the nose heals with little to no visible signs. Notice I said usually. Everyone and every broken nose is different. Badly broken and displaced noses are much more likely to have residual lumps, bumps, and scarring. If there is a cut on the nasal skin, it will leave a visible scar.

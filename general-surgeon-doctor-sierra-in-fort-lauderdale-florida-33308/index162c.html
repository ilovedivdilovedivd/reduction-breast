Is Finasteride necessary for a successful hair Transplant procedure?
Finasteride and transplant
Jul 6, 2021
Finasteride is not necessary for a successful transplant result. The goal of any hair-loss treatment such as finasteride, minoxidil, etc. is to stop or decrease ongoing hair-loss. It will not have any effect on transplanted follicles since these are less sensitive to DHT.

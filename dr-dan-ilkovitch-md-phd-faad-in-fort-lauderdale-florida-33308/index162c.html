Can Botox be used on the lines around my lips?
Botox lip lines
May 25, 2018
It’s dofficult to tell from your photo, but most lip lines may require a combination of filler and small amounts of Botox. Only have that performed by a board certified core doctor (plastics, Dermatology,..). Also we don’t recommend that musicians have Botox to this area as it can affect lip movements temporarily.

Options for lowering eyebrows, possibly with Botox? Can it be done on me?
Botox to lower eyebrows
May 23, 2018
Botox can be used to lower eyebrows, but there is a fine line as it may lead to eyelid drooping. Go with low amounts and add more later if needed. Make sure a board certified physician in the core specialties attempts this for you.

Can’t smile after Botox & filler
Can’t smile after botox
May 19, 2018
It is likely that Botox that was injected into crows feet diffused and affected the smile lip raising muscles (zygomatic muscles). The good thing is that Botox is temporary and this will wear off. For the future you may need to use less Botox to the crows feet, or make sure the doctor is aware of what happened and places it more laterally, or just avoid treating this area since you are worried about your job.
I had Botox done. It was amazing and at the 5 week mark I started to notice it wearing off
Botox wearing off fast
May 19, 2018
It’s possible that you just needed more units, if your muscles are strong they will wear it off faster if not enough placed. Would see a board certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon to make sure that they are using true Botox and diluting the product properly as well. A lot of medical spas advertise cheaper Botox because they dilute it.

My nose has changed since I had botox for a gummy smile. What is the cause?
Change in nose
Apr 11, 2016
The muscles injected to get rid of a gummy smile make the nose move as well. When this muscles are paralyzed, the nose will act differently. It will resolve when the Botox wears off.

Why does PRP require multiple treatments with short duration in between when first starting trea
PRP for hair loss
Dec 11, 2015
I usually recommend 2 series of injections spaced about 6-8 weeks apart. The hair grows in cycles and you will see more benefit by targeting hair follicles that are in different stages of the cycle. In my opinion PR works well for hair loss regrowth and loss prevention.

Can capsular contracture happen after explant and lift?
Capsular contracture after implant removal
Jul 23, 2019
Capsular contracture is a possible complication where scar tissue forms around a breast implant, causing deformity or pain. Most commonly the treatment is removal of the capsule and either removal or replacement of the implant. This problem cannot happen when there is no implant in place. If the whole capsule or the majority of the capsule was removed, you should not have any issues related to your previous implants.

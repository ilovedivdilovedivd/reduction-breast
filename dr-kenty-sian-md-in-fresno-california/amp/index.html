I have asymmetrical and sagging breast. What can I do?
Asymmetrical sagging breasts
Nov 10, 2018
You pictures show ptotic (sagging) asymmetric breasts and you can definitely benefit from a breast lift. If one breast is slightly bigger than the other, some breast tissue can be removed from the bigger breast to make them look more even. The breast lift will make the breasts look perkier and reposition your areola to place them at the center of the breast mound. Good luck.

Is it normal to feel a little depressed after breast augmentation?
Depressed after BA
Nov 4, 2018
If you are slightly depressed after your breast augmentation surgery, you may not be alone. Sometimes, patients underestimate the amount of pain and discomfort they go through after plastic surgery. What type of procedure would get rid of all of this overhang with my stomach?
Overhang/Bodytite
Aug 9, 2018
Based on the photos you have provided, the most suitable treatment for you would be an abdominoplasty (aka tummy tuck). This procedure will address the loose skin that is folding over the supra pubic crease and it also allows tightening of your abdominal wall. Don’t waste your money on “bodytite” because even if it can address the skin laxity to the same degree as a tummy tuck (I seriously doubt this), it still won’t address the laxity of your abdominal wall.

My veins are darker 4 months post-op from sclerotherapy – What should I do now?
Staining post sclerotherapy
Nov 21, 2021
The brown discoloration is called staining and may take 18 months to resolve. This can occur following sclerotherapy. Avoid sun tanning or burning over these areas and you may want to try Dermaka topically. Improvement with staining has been reported using this product. Most staining will improve over time.

Bone graft after extracting a tooth
Dec 7, 2019
If possible I always put bone graft into sockets where teeth are removed from. This helps to prevent bone reduction and gum recession and also allows me to place an implant or implants later if a patient so desires based on my recommendation. So go with it. Good luck, Dr. Daniyar
Can a wisdom tooth shift your teeth and essentially replace a missing tooth?
Wisdom teeth pushing other teeth
Dec 7, 2019
I would say “no” because even if such shifting does occur it will be incomplete and far from desirable alignment. The odds are better when second molars are extracted and then wisdom teeth are erupting in their stead, bt even then it remains grossly unpredictable process. So better not to lose your molar(s). Good luck, Dr. Daniyar

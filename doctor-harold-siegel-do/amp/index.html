Statement: Dr. Siegel is board-certified physician and hair transplant surgeon that graduated from the University of Florida in 1991 where he received a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology. He was involved with the Professional Service Organization, volunteering in the emergency room at UF Health Shands Hospital in Gainesville, Florida.

His undergraduate training prepared him for Nova Southeastern University, in Davie, FL, where he earned a degree as an Osteopathic Physician (DO) in 1996. Dr. Siegel then completed a 3-year residency in medicine at Palmetto General Hospital in Hialeah, Florida.

Over the next 16 years, he worked as a full-time Urgent Care and Emergency Medicine physician in several hospitals across South Florida. He held a full-time staff position at Broward Health in Fort Lauderdale, Florida and continues to be involved in educating medical students and residents in the oral-maxillofacial field.

Dr. Siegel also supports a successful chart review company, servicing physicians and hospitals since 1999.

Dr. Siegel was originally introduced to hair transplants by his friend and colleague, Chief Medical Director of Natural Transplants, Hair Restoration Clinic, Dr. Matt Huebner. After completing an extensive hair replacement training program, and successfully mastering our exclusive HUE (High-Yield Unit Extraction®) Method he began preforming hair loss treatment procedures at our Fort Lauderdale Clinic.

Dr. Siegel is located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida where he lives with his wife and two young children. He and his wife enjoy cruising and family weekends in sunny South Florida.

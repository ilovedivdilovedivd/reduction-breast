How long after a breast reduction can I get pregnant?
Getting Pregnant After Breast Reduction
Sep 23, 2021
Hello! It is possible to become pregnant after breast reduction provided you allow your breasts to heal for at least 1 year so that your breast tissues grow back and reconnect with any severed milk ducts and to give the nerves a chance to regenerate in a process called recanalization and reinnervation.

How Long Will It Take to Recover Nipple Sensitivity After Breast Mastectomy?
Nipple Sensitivity after Breast Mastectomy
Sep 23, 2021
Hello! It’s common to experience loss of nipple sensitivity after a mastectomy, because of the nerves that have been severed during the procedure. Unfortunately, there is no way to tell how long it will take for nipple sensitivity to return, or if it will return at all, and it’s best to consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon who can help you figure out what your current situation is and how to proceed from there.

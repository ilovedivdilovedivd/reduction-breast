Uneven eyelid and “crow’s foot” after upper blepharoplasty
Postop blepharoplasty eyelid healing
Nov 11, 2023
After blepharoplasty, like most cosmetic surgery procedures, it takes a least up to 6 months for swelling to subside and see the final results. Asymmetry in the eyelids in the early postop period; may be related to the swelling or due to underlying preoperative asymmetry. Appropriate follow up appointments with ones plastic surgeon should help answer the concerns.
Am I a candidate for eyelid surgery?
Eyelid surgery and browlift
Nov 11, 2023
Upper blepharoplasty removes the extra upper eyelid skin that causes hooding. In patients with low set eyebrows, often a brow lift is performed at the same time as upper eyelid surgery.
At times with an isolated upper blepharoplasty, the eyebrows could get lowered, therefore combining the two procedures can enhance the cosmetic results of the eye region.
Under eye hollows/bags and crepey skin
Lower eyelid bags and crepey skin
Nov 1, 2023
Lower eyelid bags are addressed with removing the excess orbital fat; at times this fat can be transferred into the tear trough to smooth the valleys. Crepey skin is carefully excised and often with a lid tightening procedure like a canthopexy. Blepharoplasty give much longer lasting results than fillers.
Do you think I will get a good result from Lipo360?
Lipo360
Nov 1, 2023
Lipo 360 you are removing the excess fat of the abdomen, flanks and back. Over several months, swelling subsides and the skin may contract in patients with good skin quality. Overhanging excess skin, muscle separation and bulge, stretch marks and poor skin quality are better treated with abdominoplasty excising the extra tissue but with longer scars. Tummy tuck is usually performed in patients with no plans for future pregnancy.
Would lipo and flexsculpt fix this?
Tummy tuck vs. abdominal liposuction
Nov 1, 2023
Tummy tuck vs. abdominal liposuction depends on several factors including skin quality, scars, diastasis recti and plans for future pregnancy.
In a tummy tuck, the excess skin and tissue is excised, muscle tightened and usually some liposuction for additional contouring.
Liposuction you are just removing the excess fat and with time the skin may contract. Bands or skin folds may persist or recur after liposuction.

Hooded eyelids
Aug 27, 2023

Fat can be placed to restore the fullness as well; filler is used much less commonly.

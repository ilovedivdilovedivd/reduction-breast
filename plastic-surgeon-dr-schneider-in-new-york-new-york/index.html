Any new creative options to be aware of for breast reconstruction?
Breast reconstruction should always be creative – and completely individualized to you!
Nov 4, 2022
Good morning,

Breast reconstruction should always be creative since we have so many options available. We are able to take tissue from your abdomen, back or other areas of your body to create a natural, soft breast. A newer development is being able to attach the nerve from that tissue to the nerve in your chest to return sensation to your breast after mastectomy surgery.

Does Breast Reconstruction DIEP Flap procedure result in loss of body weight?
A DIEP Flap procedure will change your body
Nov 4, 2022
Good morning!

A DIEP flap procedure involves moving the skin and fat from the stomach and using that to create new breasts. It will not result in weight loss because the tissue is being saved – moved to create new breasts. However, your body will look as though you had an abdominoplasty or tummy tuck.

I hope this helps!
Dr. Schneider

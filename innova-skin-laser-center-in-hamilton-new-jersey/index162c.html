Are welts after IPL a normal side effect?
Yes, they are.
May 25, 2023
Although they may not occur every time, it is not uncommon to experience welts or redness after IPL treatments. Welts can sometimes be a side effect of IPL that occur due to irritation. If the welts persist or seem to worsen severely, it is then recommended to consult your practitioner.
Is it necessary to wait between IPL and laser hair removal?
It is safest to wait at least two to four weeks.
May 25, 2023
Typically, it is recommended to wait at least two to four weeks after your last IPL treatment to begin laser hair removal sessions. This waiting period allows the skin to recover and minimizes the risk of adverse reactions, such as excessive heat or irritation during laser hair removal. Thus, it is advisable to wait for a certain period of time between these two procedures to avoid potential complications and ensure optimal results.

During an arm lift, do surgeons always have to remove a bundle of nerves?
Arm lift and nerves
Mar 10, 2022
An arm lift procedure (AKA brachioplasty) is a procedure to remove skin and fat from the upper arms. Sometimes this is also combined with liposuction. One of the potential risks of this procedure is injury to the superficial nerves that would lead to pain, tingling, or loss of sensation to the skin on the upper arm. Although this is common risk associated with this type of surgery, It is NOT common for a plastic surgeon to purposefully remove the nerve bundle.

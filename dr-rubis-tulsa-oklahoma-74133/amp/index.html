Tuberous breast correction – advice?
Lift options
Dec 17, 2020
Periareolar scars generally will become a thicker more noticeable scar in the long run. The benefit of the vertical incision is to take the tension off your scars so that they heal finer and less noticeable. It also allows better shaping to the breast. The vertical scar generally will fade to look more like a stretch mark. Good luck and best wishes!
I have wide shoulders and a big rib cage? Would a breast augmentation make the area look bigger and mor
Breast implants
Dec 17, 2020
A full or extra full silicone implant would provide you with good fullness and help to shape your breasts. You may need some shaping of your right breast to help with symmetry. As long as you stay with an implant that fits your natural breast diameter, the breasts will look proportionate and defined. If you go with a large implant that is wider than your natural breast, that is when it starts to make you look broad or “heavy”. You could have a fantastic result, as long as you pick a size…
What will get me closest to wish pics (I’m 5’5″, 126lbs, 32A, 11.7 BWD)?
Implant size
Dec 17, 2020
Cup size is very dependent on your natural breast diameter, But based off the photos, I would suggest a full profile mid to high cohesive silicone implant in the upper 200’s or low 300’s. Remember, it is easy to go bigger, harder to go smaller after the surgery is performed. Take care and best wishes for an amazing result!
I need more opinions before my surgery on over or under the muscle?
Implant Placement
Dec 17, 2020
That size of implant would be important to put under the muscle for long term results. Skin cannot support the weight of an implant long term. Under the muscle will support your implant, hide the edges of the implant providing a softer result, prevent capsular contractures, as well as having clearer mammograms. Yes, you will have a bit more discomfort the first few days, but it will be well worth it in the long run. It is always best to start on the right path, to prevent future problems….
How can I make my tuberous breasts look normal?
Breast shaping
Dec 17, 2020
HI, you do have some mild tubularity. This could be corrected with a implant and breast lift to give you fullness at the top of your breast, and shaping of the lower breast. Areola size could also be reduced to be more proportionate to your breast. It would be very important for you to have the proper diameter to your implants to ensure a nice cleavage line, due to your breast sitting a bit to the outside of your chest wall. I would recommend a mid cohesive full profile or extra full profile…
Can I get areola reduction before pregnancy? I know it’s not advised, but can I have it redone after pr
Areola reduction
Dec 17, 2020
Areola reduction can be done prior to having children and generally will not interfere with breastfeeding. Hormones and weight changes or breast size changes could lead to the areola stretching out again, but can be redone. A consultation to determine the size recommendation for your areola reduction would be recommended. During your consult, you would be informed of all risks associated with surgery and what expectations you should have.
2 weeks post op and still pretty sore with muscle spasms frequently, is that normal?
Post breast augmentation muscle tightness
Apr 11, 2019
Congratulations on your surgery. It is completely normal to continue to have muscle tightness at this point. The cycle of spasms can be different for all patients, based on muscle tightness and implant size. The roller coaster generally starts to subside around the two week mark, but can take up to several months before you get to your final result. Give it time, following your doctor’s instructions and restrictions. Ice packs can help as well. Best wishes!

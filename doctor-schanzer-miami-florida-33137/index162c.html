Botox for mentalis – Will muscle continue to shrink and atrophy?
Mentalis botox shrinkage
Mar 17, 2023
The mentalis muscle normally functions to elevate the base of the lower lip (everting and protruding it) as well as wrinkling the skin of the chin. When injected with neurotoxin (botox, dysport, jeuveau, Xeomin, daxxify) this activity will temporarily be reduced and the muscle will be weakened and atrophy (shrinking it).

How can I lose all my excess weight before tummy tuck for best and safest results?
Be close to your goal weight prior to body contouring surgery
Jul 1, 2021
It's absolutely best to be at or new your goal weight prior to surgery--your risks may be lower, your outcomes will be optimized, and you decrease the chance of needing revisions after your surgery. If your weight fluctuates significantly after TT you may be left with loose skin requiring additional surgery!

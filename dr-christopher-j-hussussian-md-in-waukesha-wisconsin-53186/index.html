Is it safe to take Adderall prior to general anesthesia for breast augmentation?
Adderall before Surgery
Aug 26, 2022
Hello
Every surgeon and anesthesiologist will have their own surgical protocol they develop. Therefore nobody here can tell you whether you will need to stop or not when having surgery with someone outside of their practice. That said, my personal protocol is to stop all medications 2 weeks prior to surgery as to not interfere with the administering of your general anesthesia.

Flying
Aug 26, 2022
Hello
There is no set agreed upon timeframe. I suggest waiting 72 hours but that is more for sutures and lifting than because of the airplane itself.

If I decide to have another baby will it ruin the look of my breast augmentation?
Breast Aug after child
Aug 26, 2022
Hello,
While the implant will not change from child birth, the breast tissue and skin around it can. Sometimes it results in a more natural look that women actually prefer. Sometimes it results in a result they do not necessarily appreciate including more sagging or separation. That is not easily predicted. But you definitely can experience changes in the appearance.
Christopher J Hussussian, MD

Surgeon used incorrect implant size: what’s the size difference for 50cc?
Implant Size
Aug 26, 2022
Hello,
50CC’s is about 3 tablespoons difference. In all honestly, switching down to a 400CC implant is not going to provide you much of a difference. If you feel that you are too big you are probably going to be guided down to 350CC or 300CC.
Revision policy’s are going to vary surgeon and office. For me, happy patients are most important so I would offer a discounted revision whether it was my fault or the patient simply chose a size they’re not happy with.
Christopher J. Hussussian, MD

Pre-operative testing
Aug 26, 2022
Hello,
Every physician is going to be different in what they suggest and require of their patients pre-operatively. Can I use lipo to treat breast asymmetry?
Breast Asymmetry
Aug 26, 2022
Hello
Breast Asymmetry can be addressed with liposuction. It is hard to tell whether it would be a good option for you without a physical exam. If prominent scarring is what you’re trying to avoid I would suggest a small aug where two sized implants are used create the symmetry you’re looking for.

Healing process for inverted nipple surgery?
Recovery from inverted nipple surgery
Mar 31, 2023
Thank you for your question. Correction of inverted nipples is a short office procedure that is done under local anesthesia. A small incision of about 1 cm is made and the tethering ducts are divided. Two absorbable sutures are then placed. Downtime typically is a week or so and full activity at two weeks. Most people who have had the procedure are surprised at how quickly the condition can be corrected after having had it for years. Consult with Board certified plastic surgeon near you.

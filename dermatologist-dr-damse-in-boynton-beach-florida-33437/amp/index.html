Please tell me if Mohs is ever appropriate for a precancerous condition?
No, Mohs cannot be used for an actinic keratosis
Feb 9, 2022
Mohs surgery is not appropriate for treating actinic keratoses. I would confirm the diagnosis with your physician – perhaps something has been lost in translation. Mohs surgery is reserved for skin cancer removal, and even all skin cancers do not qualify for this procedure. If you want to avoid scarring in this area, a topical chemotherapy cream can be used to treat actinic keratoses. I hope this helps!

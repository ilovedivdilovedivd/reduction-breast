My injector says my lips are uneven as I’m not giving it enough time. My lips were injected with Juved
Still swollen after 15 days
Apr 17, 2017
It is very common for injections into the lips to need time to even out. I am always conservatively aggressive when ti comes to lip, because it is easier to put a bit more in, than it is to take too much out. Give your lips time, and make sure you keep your injection physician in the know. She or he is the best source of information for your case.

Depressed scar on forehead due to plastic surgery fixing an existing scar. Any suggestions?
Fillers for depressed scars
Jul 30, 2016
Scar revision should utilize a long-lasting filler. I use silicone (Silikon 1000) and Bellafill. Both are long-lasting and will give a wonderful correction. You may need some ablative laser or manual derm abrasion to get the best correction. Re-excision is always an option, but you have already had that once. Try the fillers I mentioned above, for a satisfying correction.

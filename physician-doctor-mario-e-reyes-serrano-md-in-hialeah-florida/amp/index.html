What causes frown lines between eye brown at age of 25 and how to eliminate it?
Treatment for Frown Lines Between Eyebrows
Oct 17, 2017
Frown lines, such as the ones in the photo, are quite common. Many patients begin to develop frown lines in their twenties; more so if they are in the sun a lot (squinting) or if they make many facial expressions when they speak. Botox is a wonderful option to achieve the results you desire! It lasts anywhere between 4-5 months and relaxes the muscles and improves the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Side effects are not common.
